**Wild Guardy** (мЩАмЭЉлУЬк∞АлФФ, _Waildeu Gadi_) is a Cardbot in _[Metal Cardbot](</wiki/Metal_Cardbot_\(season\)> "Metal Cardbot \(season\)")_. He is a rival of [Blue Cop](</wiki/Blue_Cop> "Blue Cop") and originally planned to join the [Star Guardians](</wiki/Star_Guardians> "Star Guardians") until Machina was destroyed. After arriving on [Earth](</wiki/Earth> "Earth"), Wild Guardy met and formed a friendship with [Crest](</wiki/Crest> "Crest").

## History

On planet [Machina](</wiki/Machina> "Machina"), Wild Guardy idolized Blue Cop as the greatest Star Guardian and dreamed of becoming one himself. However, the planet was destroyed before he had a chance to. He drifted through space in an escape pod and ultimately crashed-landed on Earth, suffering terrible damage. The human [Crest](</wiki/Crest> "Crest") found him and had the technological means to repair him over time, and his kindness resulted in Wild Guardy making his first friend on Earth. After recovering, his goal shifted from becoming a Star Guardian to besting Blue Cop, the only remaining Star Guardian, and becoming the king of all Metal Cardbots.

Wild Guardy's first interaction with Jun's group would be saving them from a recently-awakened [Heavy Iron](</wiki/Heavy_Iron> "Heavy Iron"), slashing the outlaw while he was charging an attack and giving Blue Cop the chance to hit with a Mega Volt Punch to send him running. While Wild Guardy quickly vanished after doing his part, he would make another appearance at Edo Motors to challenge Blue Cop, managing to critically damage him while driving off [Mega Trucker](</wiki/Mega_Trucker> "Mega Trucker"), only choosing to leave after [Shadow X](</wiki/Shadow_X> "Shadow X") arrived.

Following his first duel with Blue Cop, Wild Guardy would mostly stick to observing him from the sidelines, interfering twice during the first encounters with [Dexter](</wiki/Dexter> "Dexter") and [Fleta Z](</wiki/Fleta_Z> "Fleta Z"), damaging the former's eyes in the first incident and ousting himself as an accomplice of Crest's in the second. Eventually, Wild Guardy would make his final challenge against Jun's group, and after taking out all but Fleta Z, Blue Cop would step in and ultimately defeat him despite the upstart's best efforts. Staying true to their prior agreement, Crest allowed Jun to seal Wild Guardy with the [Metal Breath](</wiki/Metal_Breath> "Metal Breath"), later formally passing over custody of him after encountering Heavy Iron and [Black Hook](</wiki/Black_Hook> "Black Hook") at the [Mowtown Landfill](</wiki/Mowtown_Landfill> "Mowtown Landfill").

## Appearance

Wild Guardy is a violet-blue Cardbot with a knight design, a gladiator helmet-shaped head with a gold wing on each side, yellow eyes, gold sections on the torso, silver sections on the outer shoulders, forearms, upper legs and outer lower legs, silver back, upper arms, and upper legs with a gold plate each, half a blue-violet tail fin with a silver center on each leg, light-gray hands with a gold backhand, and light-gray feet. His vehicle form is a sports car modelled after a Ford Mustang.

### Early designs

Wild Guardy was originally light blue and orange, before having his colors changed to mesh better with other Cardbots.[1]
