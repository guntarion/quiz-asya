**Buster Gallon** (버스터갤런, _Beoseuteo Gaelleon_) is a [Metal Cardbot](</wiki/Metal_Cardbots> "Metal Cardbots") in _[Metal Cardbot](</wiki/Metal_Cardbot_\(season\)> "Metal Cardbot \(season\)")_. Originally part of the [Black Hook Crew](</wiki/Black_Hook_Crew> "Black Hook Crew") led by [Black Hook](</wiki/Black_Hook> "Black Hook"), he goes rogue after [Buffalo Crush](</wiki/Buffalo_Crush> "Buffalo Crush") was sealed by [Jun Grant](</wiki/Jun_Grant> "Jun Grant"). He later joins Jun after being beaten by Buffalo Crush.

### Metal Cardbot

Episode 19: Buster Gallon appears alongside Black Hook to support Buffalo Crush, who is struggling against Jun’s group. However, Buffalo Crush, concerned about being indebted to Jun’s mother, insists that they should withdraw for the day. Buster Gallon slightly objects, but Black Hook, understanding Buffalo Crush's sense of honor, decides to take him and retreat.

Episode 20: When Black Hook asks what he plans to do with Metal Breath, he replies that he will conquer the universe, divide it into three parts, and let each of them rule their ow territory. Under Black Hook’s leadership, who was devising a way to bypass Star Guardian Blue Cop’s security, he secretly deploys three Mini Gallons to Jun’s house at night to steal the Metal Breath and monitors their movements using GPS signals. However, the next morning, as the Mini Gallons attempt to escape after failing their mission, Buffalo Crush accidentally crushes them under his wheels near the Motown Car Center.

Buster Gallon, who had been following him, bursts into tears upon seeing the shattered Mini Gallons. Just then, Buffalo Crush, who had been feigning ignorance, swings a sledgehammer but is counterattacked by Dexter. He provides covering fire with his Chest Cannon. When Black Hook and Buffalo Crush begin arguing out of frustration, he chooses to stay silent and withdraw, deciding it’s not worth the trouble.

Episode 21: Upon discovering through a flying Mini Gallon that Jun has caught a cold, he is delighted and joins his comrades in an attempt to steal the Metal Breath. However, upon arriving at Jun’s house, they find Anna and Fleta Z already waiting. When Anna spots the Metal Breath, he chases after them. However, thanks to Fleta Z’s quick thinking, they end up getting hit by guided missiles from the Chest Cannon, leading to a chaotic exchange of attacks. In the meantime, it is revealed that Anna’s Metal Breath was a fake, and Jun and the other Metal Cardbots appear with the real one. Outmatched, they retreat while taking heavy blows.

Episode 22: He is visibly flustered upon seeing Buffalo Crush suddenly embark on a solo mission. Following him with Black Hook, she attempts to threaten Jun and his family. However, when Buffalo Crush insists on handling the mission alone, she responds, "What are you talking about?" and argues that they can simply take the Metal Breath by force. After much persuasion, she ultimately decides to observe from above with Black Hook. After seeing Buffalo Crush being sealed by Jun Grant, Buster Gallon is slightly worried if things will be okay, but he is surprised to see Black Hook cry slightly as they left.

Episode 23: Fed up with Black Hook, Buster Gallon betrays him, believing that continuing the battle would take too long to steal the Metal Breath. He restrains Black Hook with a magnetic field, then detonates a grenade in the dockside warehouse, burying him alive. Afterward, he lies low nearby, quietly hiding until he hears about the explosion on the news.

When Shadow X and Fleta Z arrive from the sky to scout and control civilians, he shoots them with his Chest Cannon, rendering them incapacitated. He then single-handedly fights the other Metal Cardbots that have arrived on the ground, putting them in danger with his various inventions. However, Buffalo Crush breaks through the ice and ultimately defeats him, sealing him inside the Metal Breath. Despite his defeat, he demonstrates that the title of "genius inventor" is well-earned, having managed to incapacitate all but Buffalo Crush on his own.

Episode 24: He appears to introduce his inventions to Jun and his team, though they turn out to be the same inventions that previously gave them trouble. Later, upon learning that Black Hook is preparing for the final battle by absorbing energy from an electrical facility, he sets out to investigate. He deduces Black Hook’s plan but is completely cornered when Black Hook absorbs the surrounding energy. Realizing he cannot handle this alone, he stops assisting with the Mega Trucker’s technology maintenance. As the Metal Cardbots were mostly defeated, he seizes Jun and pretends to offer him along with Jun’s Metal Breath. However, this turns out to be a ruse—he enjoys tricking others and winks at Jun, signaling him to use his invention, the Cheating Pen. Jun plays along, using it to cause a commotion that temporarily blinds Black Hook and lowers his guard. Taking advantage of the opening, he successfully seals Black Hook by coordinating with Blue Cop, who is still able to move. Afterward, he exchanges a fist bump with Jun, promising to take good care of him. However, when Jun plays a prank with his fake arm, he is momentarily startled before laughing, admitting that he was fooled.

Episode 25: When Heavy Iron activates the Star Crown, he explains that it has a terraforming function. Upon hearing this, Anna, Ed, and Theo deduce that Earth's environment is already in disarray. They praise him for his intelligence. He later assisted in the battle against Heavy Iron, but was shocked to see his Buster Gatling shots deflected by Heavy Iron's shielding. As a last resort, he aided in Mega Trucker's Ultra Mega Power Shield, which failed and he collapsed along with the other Cardbots.

Episode 26: As Jun activated the forbidden power in the Metal Breath, Buster Gallon and the other 10 Cardbots united their powers with Blue Cop, allowing him to transform into Ultimate Blue Cop in order to defeat and seal Heavy Iron. The Machinasium generated by the Star Crown’s destruction turns into stone, though some highly concentrated fragments remain. As part of the recovery process, he monitors the situation using a tablet while secretly collecting the valuable pieces under the cover of night. He was later seen at the end with the rest of the Cardbots as Heavy Iron accepted Jun's friendship.

## Appearance

Buster Gallon is a green Cardbot with a construction worker motif, a hardhat/gas mask structured head with canister-shaped symbols on each side, green eyes, a yellow triangle symbol and half a deconstructed bulldozer blade on each forearm, black hands, yellow parts throughout his body, a white lower torso, upper arms, and upper legs, teal hip and lower legs, a black triangle symbol on each yellow knee extension, black parts on the shoulders and shins, and black feet. His vehicle form is a bulldozer/tractor hybrid vehicle.

## Arsenal & Techniques

  * **Metal Card - Buster Gatling** (메탈카드 - 버스터 개틀링, _Metal Kadeu - Beoseuteo Gaeteulling_): A translucent amber colored chest-mounted artillery weapon. Consists of a cannon on Buster Gallon's chest and a gatling gun on each shoulder.
    * **Buster Blaster** (버스터 블래스터, _Beoseuteo Beullaeseuteo_): Buster Gallon's main ultimate move, he fires a green laser beam from the Chest Cannon.
    * **Twin Vulcan** (트윈 발칸, _Teuwin Balkan_): Buster Gallon fires energy beams from the Buster Gatling's gatlings.
    * **Chest Cannon** (체스트 캐논, _Cheseuteu Kaenon_): Buster Gallon generates and fires energy shells from the Chest Cannon.
  * **[Mini Gallons](</wiki/Mini_Gallons> "Mini Gallons")** (미니 갤런, _Mini Gaelleon_): Small robots that are exactly like Buster Gallon. Each drone can be customized, and some have different colors, or GPS, drills, scissors, propellers, and even the ability to take pictures.

## Trivia

  * Despite his name being in transliterated English, his name is localized as "Busty Gallon" in the English and Indonesian dubs of the series.
  * Buster Gallon possesses a wrench that converts into a laser gun in both promotional images and the set, but he does not wields it in the show.
  * While the set possess a third form for Buster Gallon that converts him into a drone with treads, the form was never utilized in the show.
