**Flame Nova** (нФМл†ИмЮДлЕЄл∞Ф, _Peulleim Noba_) is a new [Metal Cardbot](</wiki/Metal_Cardbots> "Metal Cardbots") and a major antagonist in _[Metal Cardbot S](</wiki/Metal_Cardbot_S> "Metal Cardbot S")_. Originally a [Star Guardian](</wiki/Star_Guardians> "Star Guardians"), Flame Nova grew ambitious and rebelled against [Deus Machina](</wiki/Deus_Machina> "Deus Machina") for power, but was captured and branded a criminal. On Earth, Flame Nova formed an alliance with the [Hidden Cardbots](</wiki/Hidden_Cardbots> "Hidden Cardbots") while setting his sights on [Mukara](</wiki/Mukara> "Mukara")'s [Metal Breath](</wiki/Metal_Breath> "Metal Breath") to regain his true form and enact his plan of turning the [Earth](</wiki/Earth> "Earth") into a home for the Metal Cardbot species. His plan failed by the strength of Cardbots coexisting with humans, and he was sealed by [Jun Grant](</wiki/Jun_Grant> "Jun Grant").

In _[Metal Cardbot W](</wiki/Metal_Cardbot_W> "Metal Cardbot W")_ , Flame Nova closed himself off to prevent Jun from summoning him, which the latter decide to wait until Flame Nova chose to open up to humans.

## History

Flame Nova was once a distinguished member of the Star Guardians and [Blue Cop](</wiki/Blue_Cop> "Blue Cop")'s superior, renowned as a courageous agent who had received the Machina Cross award three times. He was also the Cardbot responsible for taking down the Wild Cardbot [Solidhorn](</wiki/Solidhorn> "Solidhorn"), defeating him single-handedly before locking him up. However, one day, he rebelled against Deus Machina, attempting to steal the memory core by infiltrating the sanctuary on Planet Machina, stealing three sets of [Cosmic Stones](</wiki/Cosmic_Stone> "Cosmic Stone") and discovering the first Metal Cardbot resides within Machina. His rebellion failed and he was declared a felon for violating the sanctuary. He was expelled from the Star Guardians and sentenced to imprisonment. His body was fragmented, and his ability to combine into robot mode was permanently sealed by the power of the Metal Breath. Without an escape pod, Flame Nova was presumed dead when Planet Machina exploded. However, he survived and took refuge on Earth.

Flame Nova is a red and black Cardbot with a gestalt body equal in height to [Heavy Iron](</wiki/Heavy_Iron> "Heavy Iron"), [Gigantrex](</wiki/Gigantrex> "Gigantrex"), [Blue Cop Trinity](</wiki/Blue_Cop> "Blue Cop"), [Sky Gallop](</wiki/Sky_Gallop> "Sky Gallop") and [Blastrain](</wiki/Blastrain> "Blastrain"), a black head similar to Blue Cop Trinity's sporting a hat-style, flaps, a golden flame medal on the forehead, and a flame symbol on each side, a blue visor, green eyes with translucent pupils; they were initially solid black before his betrayal, large shoulders, a red torso with a silver grille and front bumper on the chest and wheels along the sides, a white lower torso with a black center, a red back sporting a circle vent and main vehicle component's rear wheels, white upper arms, asymmetrical forearms (the right being black and the left more red), white hands with a black palm and red backhand, a hip with black armor sporting red centers and red side flaps, white upper legs, red lower legs with gold sections, green/white/black knee extensions and treads lining up with the outer calves, and black feet with a white base each. ## Peronality

Of all the Metal Cardbots introduced in the first 2 seasons, Flame Nova is by far the most malicious of the entire species, not only outright refusing to coexist with humans but being willing to betray his own people, with a past attempt at doing so being what cost him his position in the Star Guardians. Additionally, Flame Nova has a high superiority complex and does not like being unable to control things to his exact liking, being fully willing to enslave his own race under his thumb.

## Trivia

  * Flame Nova's initial debut at the end of episode 19 had him sporting a yellow coloration, which was changed to red and black in episode 20.
  * Like Blue Cop Trinity, Flame Nova is a gestalt comprised of multiple vehicles.
  * This is the first Metal Cardbot in the _Metal Cardbot_ series to have a combined form as its base form.
  * As of the finale of _Metal Cardbot S_ , Flame Nova is the only Cardbot to be an unrepentant villain.
  * Unlike the preceding Cardbots, Flame Nova's Metal Card is not any of his primary colors. Instead, it is the primary color of his Card Weapon.
  * The Flame Nova set is the first in the _Metal Cardbot_ toy line to utilize rotating axis to hide both eyes when transforming from vehicle into robot mode (this is due to necessity, as the nature of his design means that without this feature, Flame Nova's vehicle mode eyes would be constantly visible in robot mode outside of a direct shot of his front and back).
  * Flame Nova shares numerous similarities with Red Blitz in personality, namely being both stubborn, aggressive, and willing to fight over any minor acts of defiance. The key difference, however, is that Red Blitz both has genuinely benevolent intentions and can be convinced to put his annoyances aside when he needs to, whereas Flame Nova is both actively malicious and puts his own goals before everyone else.
